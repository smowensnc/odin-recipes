Korean BBQ Chicken Marinade

This Korean chicken marinade can also be used as a basting sauce or served over chicken rice bowls. It's very tasty! If you like it hot, increase the hot chile paste as desired up to four tablespoons â€” that's about as much as anyone can take!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 4 teaspoons hot chile paste

Directions

  1. Whisk sugar, soy sauce, water, onion powder, and ground ginger together in a medium saucepan over high heat. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, and simmer for 5 minutes.
  2. Remove from the heat and let cool to room temperature, 20 to 30 minutes. Whisk in lemon juice and chile paste.

To Use:

Pour marinade over chicken, cover, and marinate in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours. Then cook chicken as desired.
